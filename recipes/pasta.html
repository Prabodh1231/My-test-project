Pasta

Description

Juicy sweet tomatoes burst open in warm olive oil, creating a luxuriously silky sauce that comes together in minutes.

This recipe takes about 20-25 min in preparation for a 4 serving

Ingredient

  • 12 ounces linguine
  • 1½ pounds beefsteak tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 large garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ cup fresh basil, chopped
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan (1 ounce)
  • Lots of love

Directions

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente; drain and transfer to a large bowl.
  2. Meanwhile, in a food processor, puree the tomatoes, oil, garlic, ¾ teaspoon salt, and ¼ teaspoon pepper.
  3. Toss the pasta with the tomato sauce and half the basil. Sprinkle with the Parmesan and the remaining basil just before serving.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving 437 calories; protein 15g; carbohydrates 68g; sugars 7g; fiber 4g; fat 11g; saturated fat 2g; sodium 494mg; cholesterol 5mg.
